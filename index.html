16 Weeks To Design A Robot From Scratch

Participants will have 8 weeks to CAD and code their own subemersible device, vehicle, or robot using either a SeaPerch ROV Kit or a $200 grant.
They will then attend the event and receive their robot kit or items within their Bill Of Materials depending on what option they chose.
